# ADR-006: MIT license

**Status:** Accepted | **Date:** 2026-03-04

## What we decided
tapes is released under the MIT license.

## Why
Maximum compatibility and zero friction for adoption. The realistic risk of a company taking this tool proprietary and harming users is negligible for a personal media organiser. beets, the closest reference tool, also uses MIT.

## Alternatives considered
- **GPL v3**: ensures derivatives stay open source. Rejected: the copyleft protection isn't worth the friction for this type of tool.
- **Apache 2.0**: adds explicit patent grants. Rejected: no meaningful patent risk here, and MIT is simpler.
